How to Revise Maths for GCSE

GCSE Maths is best revised by doing questions, not rereading notes.

Step 1: Identify weak topics

List topics you struggle with, such as algebra, fractions, or graphs.

Step 2: Learn the method, then practise

Understand the method first, then answer lots of questions.

Step 3: Use past questions

Past GCSE-style questions train you for the exam.
