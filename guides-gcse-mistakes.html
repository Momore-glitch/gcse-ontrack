GCSE Revision Mistakes to Avoid

If revision isn’t working, it’s usually not because you’re lazy. It’s because your method is wasting time.

1) Rereading notes like it’s revision

Fix: Use active recall. Test yourself. Then check gaps.

2) Highlighting everything

Fix: Highlighting feels productive, but it doesn’t prove you can remember it. Turn key points into questions.

3) Cramming the night before

Fix: Use spaced repetition. Small reviews over time beat panic revision.

4) Doing “easy topics” because it feels good

Fix: Spend more time on weak areas. Comfort revision gives comfort grades.

5) Planning too much and studying too little

Fix: Keep planning simple. Use a plan that turns into daily actions.

6) No exam-question practice

Fix: Do questions regularly. Learn how marks are awarded, not just facts.
