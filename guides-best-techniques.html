Best GCSE Revision Techniques

Most students revise. Fewer revise effectively. These techniques are simple and reliable.

1) Active recall (the #1 technique)

Test yourself. Don’t just reread. Your brain remembers what it retrieves.

Read the active recall guide

2) Spaced repetition (so you don’t forget)

Revisit topics over time. Small reviews beat cramming.

Read the spaced repetition guide

3) Past-paper questions (exam skill)

Knowing content is one thing. Scoring marks is another. Practise GCSE-style questions regularly.

4) Interleaving (mix topics)

Don’t spend hours on one topic. Mix topics so your brain learns the difference between them.

5) A realistic plan (consistency wins)

If you have to decide what to do every day, you’ll procrastinate. Let a plan decide for you.
