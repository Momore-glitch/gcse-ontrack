How to Make a GCSE Revision Timetable

A good timetable is realistic, flexible, and focused on weak areas.

Step 1: Start small

Plan 1–2 hours per day, not 5–6.

Step 2: Prioritise weak subjects

Spend more time on what you find hardest.

Step 3: Mix topics

Don’t revise the same subject for too long.
