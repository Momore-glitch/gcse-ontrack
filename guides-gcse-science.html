How to Revise Science for GCSE

GCSE Science is about understanding concepts and applying them to questions.

Step 1: Learn the core ideas

Focus on key concepts in Biology, Chemistry, and Physics before memorising details.

Step 2: Use active recall

Test yourself regularly instead of rereading notes.

Step 3: Practise exam questions

Use GCSE-style questions to learn how marks are awarded.
